About Trekker

On Trekker you'll find everything from easy day hikes to moderately strenuous hikes and extended overnight backpacking trips. We have thousands of nature trails, lake hikes and river hikes. Find hikes to viewpoints and mountain summits, urban hiking trails and backcountry treks — something for hikers of all abilities and experience levels.

Every hiking trail is linked to a map and a trail overview page, which offers additional features like photos and trail reviews. Start browsing for trails and find the perfect hiking trail near you!
